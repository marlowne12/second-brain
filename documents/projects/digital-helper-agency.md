**Website:** digital-helper.com  
**Status:** Active Development  
**Owner:** Marlon

## Overview

A solopreneur digital agency targeting local service businesses (plumbers, HVAC, landscapers, etc.) with AI-powered marketing services.

## Service Offerings

### Core Services
1. **Google Business Profile Management** — Optimization, posting, review management
2. **Local SEO** — On-page optimization, citation building, rankings tracking
3. **Reputation Management** — Review monitoring, response automation
4. **Social Media** — Content creation and scheduling

### Differentiators
- **AI-Powered Efficiency** — Max handles research, content, and monitoring
- **Value-First Approach** — Build spec sites before pitching
- **Local Focus** — Deep understanding of local business needs
